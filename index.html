Imjin War 1592-98

Imjin war is the Japanese invasions of Korea (a.k.a. 임진왜란, 壬辰倭亂) launched by Toyotomi Hideyoshi in 1592-98. It is not only the most historically important war in Korea but also unforgettable scar. This project named Imjin War is a board game of the conflict on Korea peninsula between Joseon, Japan and Ming dynasty.
